Why This Matters

My intention for this website was to create a beginner-friendly resource/tool designed to help people think more carefully before buying a betta fish. The website is built around a simple quiz and supporting information pages that guide users through some of the most important care questions, such as tank size, setup, water conditions, and general readiness and more. Instead of encouraging impulse buying, the goal of the project is to slow that process down and help users understand whether they are actually prepared.

Betta fish are often treated as easy “starter” pets (there is no such thing as a starter pet!!!), even though proper care still requires planning, equipment, time, and basic knowledge. A lot of people buy them without fully understanding what they need, which can lead to poor living conditions and preventable harm. The quiz is meant to act as a starting point as it helps users reflect on whether they have the right setup and whether there are things they still need to learn before buying a betta fish or really any fish. The LEARN / SUPPORT section then gives more detailed information connected to those topics, while the References section provides the sources behind the information included on the site.

Rather than framing betta fish as low-maintenance purchases, my website is supposed to encourage users to see them as living animals that deserve proper care and preparation.

It is NOT just a fish.

All sentient beings, no matter how small, unfamiliar or “creepy”, deserve profound respect. Bettas experience the world differently from we do, and their well being depends on the environment we create for them. This kind of care reflects empathy, responsibility and a commitment to animal welfare.
